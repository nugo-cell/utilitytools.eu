FAQ

Why does the output look like boxes on my phone?

Some operating systems lack a font that covers Mathematical Alphanumeric Symbols (U+1D400–U+1D7FF). The text is still correct Unicode.

Will it work on Instagram, X and Discord?

Yes — all three render the Mathematical Alphanumeric block, which is why "fancy" bios and usernames work.
