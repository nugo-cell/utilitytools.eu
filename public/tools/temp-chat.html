Temp Chat 🔒 end-to-end encrypted · 🔥 ephemeral

Create a private chat room, share the link with up to 9 friends, talk in real-time. Messages are AES-GCM encrypted in your browser before they reach our server — and nothing is ever stored. Close the tab and the conversation is gone forever.

Start a chat

Create a room and share the link. Up to 10 people per room. The encryption key lives only in the URL fragment (the part after #) — your browser does not send it to our server, so we cannot read your messages even if we wanted to.

🔒 How the encryption works: When you create a room, your browser generates a random 256-bit AES-GCM key and puts it in the URL fragment (#room-key). Browsers never send fragments to servers, so the key only travels through whoever you share the link with. Every message is encrypted on your device, sent as opaque ciphertext, and decrypted on the recipients' devices.
🔥 Ephemeral by design: Messages are only kept in the live browser tab. Our server holds the room in RAM until everyone leaves, then erases it. There is no database, no logs, no backups. If everyone closes the tab, the conversation is gone.
