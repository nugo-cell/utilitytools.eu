Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Our core privacy principle

We believe utility tools should respect your privacy. Every tool on this site runs entirely inside your browser. When you format JSON, generate a password, build a CV, or convert a document, the data never leaves your device.

What data we collect

By default, this Service does not use tracking cookies, third-party analytics, ad networks, fingerprinting, or any form of user identification. We do not build user profiles.

Basic server access logs may be collected by the hosting provider (e.g., IP addresses, timestamps, browser type) for the sole purpose of security and availability. These logs are not linked to any user account and are rotated on a short schedule.

Local storage

Some tools may use the browser's localStorage to remember your preferences (e.g., your most recent CV draft). This data stays on your device and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser storage.

Third-party libraries

Certain tools load JavaScript libraries from public content delivery networks (CDNs). The CDN provider may log your IP address when you fetch the script. We do not share any personal information with these CDNs; they only see a request for a public file.

Cookies

This Service does not set cookies for tracking, advertising, or analytics purposes. If cookies are introduced later (for example, to remember a theme preference), they will be strictly necessary and described here.

Your data, your control

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to request, export, or delete. If you feel otherwise, please contact us and we will investigate.

Children's privacy

This Service is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent change.
