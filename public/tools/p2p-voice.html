P2P Voice Call 🔒 end-to-end encrypted

Free, private 1-to-1 voice-only call. Just a microphone — no camera, no app, no account. Audio flows directly between your two browsers, encrypted end-to-end via WebRTC. Want video? Use the video call tool.

Start a voice call

Create a new private room and send the link to the other person. Up to 2 people per room. Your microphone is required; the camera is never asked for.

🔒 What "encrypted" means here: WebRTC mandates DTLS-SRTP encryption for all audio between peers. The relay server only forwards a few signaling messages so the two browsers can find each other — it never sees, stores, or has the keys for your call. Hosted on DigitalOcean Frankfurt 🇩🇪 · GDPR-friendly · zero logs.

How it works: WebRTC opens a direct, encrypted audio channel between the two browsers. Our server only relays a small "introduction" (SDP/ICE) so the browsers can find each other through routers/firewalls. We use Google's free public STUN servers. If you're behind a strict corporate or double-NAT mobile network, the connection may fail — try a different network or a personal hotspot.
