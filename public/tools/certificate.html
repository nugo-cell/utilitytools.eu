Fake Certificate Generator

Make a custom award certificate with four design templates — Classic, Modern, Luxury and Fun. Download as PNG or print straight from the browser.

⚠ Note: These certificates are decorative — for gag gifts, classroom rewards, team-building or mock awards. They have no legal authority.

How to use

  1. Fill in the recipient name, title, reason, date and signer.
  2. Pick one of the four design templates — the preview updates live.
  3. Click Download PNG to save a 1400×990 px image you can email, post, or print at home.
  4. Or click Print and the browser's print dialog will open with only the certificate visible.

FAQ

Are these certificates legally valid?

No — they're decorative. Use for gag gifts, classroom rewards, team-building or mock awards.

Is anything uploaded to a server?

No. The certificate is rendered entirely on a Canvas in your browser; nothing leaves your device.

Can I print directly without downloading?

Yes. Click Print — the page is configured so only the certificate prints.

What size is the PNG?

1400×990 pixels — a 7:5 landscape ratio that prints cleanly on US Letter and A4 paper.
